# Looky Studio is out of beta

We're calling it: **Looky Studio is leaving beta**. The product is now generally available at [looky.studio](https://looky.studio).

## What it is

Looky Studio is a reporting platform built around an awkward truth — the same dataset usually has to serve three very different audiences:

- The **teams** that produce the numbers and need flexible dashboards to dig in.
- The **managers** that consume them weekly and want clear answers without having to maintain anything.
- The **leadership** layer above, that wants an executive KPI brief without parsing twelve tabs.

Most reporting tools try to fit all three into the same view and end up failing all three. Looky's choice is the opposite: one governed analytics workflow, three audience-shaped surfaces — **dashboards**, **guided reports**, and **executive KPI briefs** — produced from the same underlying data, kept in sync automatically.

## Why we shipped it

We spent the beta refining the parts that only show themselves when real organizations push real reporting through them: the cadence of delivery, the governance around shared metrics, and the discipline of keeping the three audience views consistent without manual hand-offs. Going GA means we're confident enough in that workflow to commit to it.

## Where to start

Tour the product at [looky.studio](https://looky.studio). The landing page walks through what each of the three audiences sees and how the reporting flows from raw data into the executive brief. If reporting overhead is a recurring drag in your organization, this is the part of the product we'd point you at first.
